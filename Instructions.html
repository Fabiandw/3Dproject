Instructions:

Movement
Use W to go forward,
use A to turn left,
use S to go backwards,
use D to turn right.

Goal
The goal of the game is to collect both of the keys.
The keys are placed in big rooms.
To collect the keys you simply walk into them.
Once you have collected both of the keys, you have to go look for the end room.
Once you put the keys in the keyhole, the game has finished.
